Medline Associate Product Mangement

Vascular Access Division



During my time in Chicago, I worked as an Associate Product Manager Intern at Medline from June to August 2024, gaining hands-on experience in market analysis, product development, and strategic sourcing. I led a $1.1B IV pump market analysis using SAP and Tableau, collaborating with Senior Engineers and Shanghai’s global sourcing team to pitch a cost-effective acquisition strategy, excluding options over 510K distribution agreements.

I partnered with APMs to develop a Hemodialysis kit composition, leveraging DART and Workfront to establish Build of Materials, pricing, and marketing strategies, laying the foundation for a 2025 product launch. Additionally, I optimized CVC kit materials based on clinical preference and cost efficiencies, achieving $40K in savings while improving usability for nurses and surgeons.

This role gave me a reality check on product management it’s not about being the “CEO of the product,” but about influencing without authority, balancing stakeholder needs with strategic priorities, and making data-driven decisions under uncertainty. I learned to align cross-functional teams around a shared vision while navigating competing priorities—an essential skill for execution-focused leadership. Managing multiple initiatives also reinforced the importance of time management, focusing on high-impact tasks, and protecting deep work time to drive meaningful outcomes.
