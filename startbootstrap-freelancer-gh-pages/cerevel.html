Cerevel Therapeutics

Business Development and Toxicology Safety Assessment Intern



During my time at Cerevel Therapeutics as a Toxicology Safety Assessment Intern in Cambridge, MA (Summer 2023), I gained valuable exposure to both scientific research and business development in the biotech space. I conducted comprehensive non-clinical safety assessments for novel CNS compounds, evaluating potential abuse risks to ensure safety and efficacy. I also assisted in FDA submissions for the KORA program, which targets depression and anxiety disorders, leveraging DICE software for structural similarity assessments.

On the business development side, I identified 18 neuroscience-focused biotech companies, 70% of which were investing in six key unmet-need areas, backed by 100+ investors. Through trend analysis, I provided strategic insights into potential partnerships, deepening my understanding of biotech investment trends, competitive landscapes, and early-stage collaborations. I learned how strategic alliances drive innovation in biotech and how companies assess pipeline synergies, market gaps, and investor interest when evaluating partnerships.

Beyond my professional work, I engaged with the Cambridge community, volunteering in a 5K event and participating in a North Point Park cleanup near North Station and TD Garden.
