BRANDA app



As the Business Head of Human Resourcing for Branda, our universities app designed for students, I have played a pivotal role in growing and leading our team while navigating a major technological transition. When our new generation of developers inherited the app, we faced a significant challenge—Branda was originally coded in React, Node.js, and Java, but our team made the shift to Swift, only to find pseudo-code embedded within the system. Despite being a business major, I took on the responsibility of not only attracting top talent but also leading the team in resolving these technical hurdles.

To build a strong foundation, I personally interviewed over 60 candidates, expanding our team from five members to a thriving 25-person team, now structured into specialized departments such as AI, UI/UX, backend, and frontend. Beyond recruitment, I focus on mentorship and engagement, ensuring every member feels valued, supported, and empowered to contribute.

Additionally, I coordinate Branda's industry outreach, organizing a Tech Trek visit to Toast Headquarters and Datadog, providing our team with invaluable insights into the tech world. Our vision has also expanded into Apple Vision Pro (AVP) development, where my co-president Ivy and I successfully secured $11,000 in funding for a spatial campus tour experience in partnership with the Admissions Office. This initiative allows international students—who often face time and financial constraints when visiting in person—to explore our campus through an immersive 3D experience, making Brandeis more accessible to prospective students worldwide.
