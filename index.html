About

Hi, I'm Gerald Deng, I am an undergraduate student on full scholarship at Brandeis University majoring in Healthcare Policy and Business with a passion for solving complex problems at the intersection of finance, healthcare, and commercial real estate.

Even though I’m not a computer science major, my curiosity got the best of me—so I rolled up my sleeves and coded a website anyway. Besides that my curiosity drives me to explore how strategic decision-making, market dynamics, and innovative solutions can shape industries and create value.

Being a competitive swimmer, I bring the same discipline, resilience, and relentless pursuit of excellence to my academic, professional, and personal endeavors. Whether tackling financial models, analyzing market trends, or managing projects, I approach every challenge with grit, adaptability, and a commitment to continuous learning.

Beyond my coursework, I actively seek opportunities I am deeply passion about. Below you can find my dedication to impactful work.
